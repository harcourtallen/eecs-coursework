American Literature

Welcome to American Literature! We were created by Aaron Ezis to share a love of literature with people around the world! He built it page-by-page, adding "A Chapter A Day" to the American Literature Library. He is remembered fondly by his family and friends who keep up this page in loving memory of him.

Aaron's Story Lesson of the Day!

From Little Red Riding Hood: The story ends with Little Red Cap vowing to never leave the path again and to always obey her mother. Thus the theme of always obeying one's parents is perhaps the strongest theme and the one best supported by the ending of the story. The lesson we learn from this story is to listen to your parents, and always obey them.
