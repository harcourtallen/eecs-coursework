Stories for Children

Please enjoy these great stories, fairy-tales, fables, and nursery rhymes for children. They help kids learn to read and make excellent bedtime stories! We have hundreds of great children's stories for you to share.

The Three Little Pigs

Three pigs build houses out of various materials to try to defend themselves from the Big Bad Wolf.

The Velveteen Rabbit

A stuffed rabbit wants to be loved by his owner but unfortunately he is forgotten in the nursery.

Jack and the Beanstalk

Jack climbs a beanstalk to a land where giants live and gets himself in trouble by stealing from the giants.

Little Red Riding Hood

A little girl in a red hood goes to visit her grandmother and encounters a surprise guest along the way.

Snow White

A beautiful women runs away from her step mother and lives with seven dwarfs in a cottage in the woods.

Cinderella

A girl living with two evil stepsisters and an evil stepmother goes to a ball and meets her Prince Charming.
